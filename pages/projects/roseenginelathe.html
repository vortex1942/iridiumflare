Rose Engine Lathe

Project Overview

The Primary goal of this project is to create a Rose Engine Lathe lathe at a lower cost than purchasing. To manufacture my own watch dials.

Design Challenges

  • Manufacture using 3D printed & Lasercut Parts
  • Use easily purchased parts where possible (608ZZ Bearings, Rods)
  • Avoid using Mills or Lathes to manufacture parts
