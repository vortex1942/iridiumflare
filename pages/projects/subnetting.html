IPv4 Subnetting

Project Overview

The Primary goal of this project was to create a basic instructional video on how to calculate IPv4 Subnets for University.
I later recieved a High Distinction for this subject

Key Challenges

  • Under 4 minutes runtime
  • Sufficiently covering a certain topic
  • Not inducing death by powerpoint on viewers

Content Covered

  • Overview of Octects, Subnets, & CIDR/DD Masks
  • Network, Host counts, & Ranges
  • Broadcast & Network IDs
  • Applying those to a full block of subnets
