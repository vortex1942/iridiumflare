Quiver Dampeners

Project Overview

The goal of this project was to solve the annoying sound of listening to arrows rattling in a quiver while walking around

First Iteration

My First iteration reduced the sound but the arrow shafts were still able to rattle which allowed room for improvement.

Second Iteration

After a CGP Grey video on hexagons I decided to redesign the part, The new design eliminated the rattle, and still allowed for 7 arrows to be held.
