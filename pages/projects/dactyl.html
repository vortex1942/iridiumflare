Dactyl-CC Keyboard

Project Overview

This was a short project to make an ergonomic keyboard, Consisting of almost 200 solder joins it took me 8 hours just to wire both keyboards

Key Challenges

During my testing process I discovered a hardware fault with the controllers, which meant the keyboards could not communicate with eachother. After a few hours of troubleshooting I found a solution. and started to wire the keyboards.

Lessons Learnt

Always test equipment prior to installation.
